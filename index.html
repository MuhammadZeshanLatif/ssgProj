About LDC

Recognizing the need for professional training in downstream gas industry, Sui Southern Gas Company (SSGC).
LDC is a fully equipped & integrated institute, with facilities for academic & practical trainings with emphasis on latest developments in the core disciplines.
We are a professionally run new age training academy established especially for the oil & gas sector.
We are responsive, creative and committed training outfit.
We are composed of mentors and consultants specializing in various aspects of corporate soft skills.

Our Mission

WE ARE COMMITTED TO TRAIN THE SSGC EMPLOYEES AND EXECUTIVE AND DEVELOP THEIR SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES WITH DISTINGUISHED KNOWLEDGE SO THAT THEY ARE EMPLOYABLE AND PROFICIENT IN THEIR CHOSEN SPECIALIZED FIELD.

Over View

Sui Southern Gas Company (SSGC) established the Learning and Development Center (LDC) Karachi in 2002 & LDC Hyderabad 2010, recognizing the need for specialized training of the Executives and employees.

RULES AND REGULATION FOR TRAINING PARTICIPANTS

LEARNING SKILLS FOR GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT

The following instructions must be followed during training session:

  1. Participants must mark their attendance in Biometric & Attendance Sheet (in and out time).
  2. Training Timing is 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  3. Be punctual (in time), 10 minutes before the start of the class.
  4. After 9:15 AM not allowed in training class.
  5. Tea break 10 minutes, Lunch Break 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM, Tea Break 10 minutes. (Participants should come back to class timely).
  6. Participants must show their interest in training. If not, Trainer may report to HR and concerned department.
  7. Discipline must be maintained in LDC during sessions.
  8. If any participant slips away from training, SSGC-LDC will take strict action.
  9. Keep mobile phone on silent/vibration mode. Only emergency calls may be attended outside.
  10. Vegetarians are requested to inform LDC for menu adjustments.
  11. LDC management will act on any misconduct or unsatisfactory behavior.
  12. Smoking, betel nut/leaf, and other prohibited items are not allowed. Violation will incur Rs.500 fine.
  13. Monthly Training Calendar is available on SSGC Intranet. Emails are also sent to department heads for nominations.
  14. Keep your class/LDC clean.
  15. Technical staff must wear uniforms during class and on-job training (if provided).
  16. Library facility is available during breaks.
  17. Absence without intimation/email will result in earned leave deduction or advisory letter.
  18. Emergency leaves must be informed via email from Department Head to GM (LDC).
  19. Repeat training in the same year is not allowed. Refresher is allowed after one year.
  20. Handle all lab equipment carefully during training.
  21. Wear PPE in technical training sessions as per HSE policy.
  22. Complete/submit your office tasks beforehand to focus fully on the training.
  23. Suggestions for improvement are always welcomed.
